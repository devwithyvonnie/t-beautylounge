Policies

Your beauty service is reserved especially for you. We value your business and ask that you respect our new policies in place.

Booking/Cancellation Policy:
Credit card information is required at time of booking. No shows and late cancellations will be charged to your card on file.

For ALL cosmetic injections and IV therapy appointment(s), we ask for $50 NON-REFUNDABLE deposit, which will be deducted from the total of service(s) provided.

Should you need to cancel or reschedule, for any reason, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance. If you are more than 5 minutes late to your appointment, it will be cancelled and you will be charged a no-show fee of 25% of the services booked. Any cancellation with less than 24 hours of notice will be subjected to a cancellation fee of 25% of the services booked.

If your card is DECLINED, you will be sent an invoice and you MUST pay it before said date or it will be sent to collections.

-You will be prohibited from booking another appointment until this balance is paid-

Fill Policy:
T-Beauty Lounge do NOT take other artist's work (foreign fills). If you booked a fill and we do not have record of you in our system, we will cancel your appointment and we will notify you to book the correct service.

If it has been more than 3 weeks since your last lash fill, it will be considered a fullset. Although, we understand that it may seem that your lashes stil look full beyond the 3 weeks mark, lashes need maintenance to ensure your lashes are happy and healthy. We must remove lashes that are grown out to prevent them from causing damage to the natural lash.

Fix Policy:
T-Beauty Lounge guarantees 100% of their work.

On the rare occasion that there is an issue with your lash extension, you MUST report to us (T-Beauty Lounge), within 5 days of your previous appointment.
